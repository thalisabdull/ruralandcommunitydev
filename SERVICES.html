Our Services:

We are committed to transforming rural communities by providing vital services that promote sustainable development and improve livelihoods. Our key services include:

  • Development and maintenance of rural infrastructure such as roads, markets, schools, and healthcare facilities.
  • Empowerment programs for youth and women, focusing on agriculture and small business support.
  • Implementation of community-driven projects to ensure inclusive growth and development.
  • Establishment of grazing reserves and support for nomadic pastoralists to promote peaceful coexistence.
  • Facilitation of agricultural development funds and training to boost productivity and economic participation.

    • Together, we work to create thriving rural communities that contribute meaningfully to the overall growth of Niger State.
