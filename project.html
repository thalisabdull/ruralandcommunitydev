Finished and Ongoing Projects:

The Niger State Ministry of Rural and Community Development has successfully completed numerous projects aimed at uplifting rural communities, including the construction and rehabilitation of rural roads, establishment of primary healthcare centers, and development of educational facilities.
Currently, we are actively implementing several key initiatives such as expanding rural infrastructure, launching youth and women empowerment programs, setting up community grazing reserves, and rolling out agricultural development funds. These projects are designed to foster sustainable growth, improve livelihoods, and ensure inclusive development across all local government areas.
